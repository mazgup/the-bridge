# The Bridge — User Guide

> For the platform's current production users.

## Getting Access
The Bridge is **invite-only**. To join:
1. Ask an admin for an invite link.
2. Visit the link — you'll see a confirmation screen.
3. Click **"Sign In with Google"** and sign in with your Google account.
4. You'll be automatically redirected to the platform.

> If you already have an account, go to the site directly and sign in — no invite needed for subsequent logins.

---

## AI CV Builder

### Starting Out
1. When you land on the CV Builder, you'll see your **CV Gallery** — a list of all your CVs.
2. Click **"New CV"** to start a fresh CV with the AI.
3. The AI will ask for your name, email, phone number, and location first.

### The AI's Approach
The AI adapts to your background automatically:
-   **If you're returning after a break or career change** → It asks about your story and elevates your experience into professional language.
-   **If you're a graduate or student** → It treats your degree like a job, expanding on modules and projects.
-   **If you're a mid-level professional** → It focuses on what makes you different from others with your title.
-   **If you're a senior executive** → It interrogates your metrics — revenue, team size, budget.

Just answer naturally. The AI does the translation.

### Building Your CV
The conversation follows a structured flow:
1. **Contact** — Name, email, phone, location.
2. **Experience** — Most recent role first. The AI will ask follow-up questions.
3. **Education** — Qualifications, school name, years.
4. **Skills** — The AI will group these by category.
5. **Summary** — The AI writes a professional summary for you.
6. **Review** — Check the PDF and request changes.

### Live PDF Preview
-   Your CV appears in real-time on the right side of the screen.
-   Switch between **Oxford Strict** (Classic, formal) and **Modern Impact** (contemporary, tech/creative) templates at any time.
-   The layout automatically adjusts to fit your content on one page.

### Saving Your Work
Your CV is **automatically saved** as you chat. If you close the browser and come back, your last session will be restored.

### Managing Multiple CVs
-   From the Gallery, you can have **multiple CV versions** (e.g., one for Finance roles, one for Tech roles).
-   Click a CV card to load it, or use the delete option to remove one.

## Admin Users Only

### Invite Management
1. Go to **Admin Dashboard** → **Invites** tab.
2. Click **"Generate Invite"** to create a new link.
3. Copy the link and send it to the person you want to invite.
4. Once used, the invite is automatically marked as used and cannot be reused.
5. You can delete unused invites at any time.

### Viewing User CVs
1. Go to **Admin Dashboard** → **Users** tab.
2. Click **"View"** next to any user.
3. You'll see a read-only replica of their builder interface — their current CV and the template they're using.

---

## Giving Feedback
Use the **Feedback** button (floating at the bottom-right of every page) to report issues or suggest improvements. This goes directly to the admin team.
