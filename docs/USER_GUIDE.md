# The Bridge - User Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to **The Bridge**, your professional sanctuary. This platform is designed to accelerate your career, whether you're climbing the ladder or returning to the workforce.

## Dashboard
Your command center.
-   **Readiness Gauge:** Shows your "Day-One Ready" score based on CV, Interview, and Briefing activity.
-   **Quick Stats:** Breakdowns of your progress.
-   **Navigation:** Use the glass cards to access tools.

## Feature Modules

### 1. CV Studio
-   **CV Audit:** Upload your current CV. Get a score, detailed feedback, and rewrite suggestions.
    -   **Templates:** Choose from Classic, Modern, Skills, or Executive styles.
    -   **Co-Pilot:** Use the AI sidebar for real-time improvements.
    -   **LinkedIn Sync:** Generate optimized LinkedIn content from your CV.
-   **CV Builder:** Build a new CV from scratch.
    -   **Chat Mode:** Have a conversation with an AI recruiter.
    -   **Skills-Based Mode:** Fill in a structured form focusing on skills and achievements.

### 2. Interview Simulation Lab
Practice makes perfect.
-   **Simulation:** Choose a role and scenario. The AI will interview you. You can type or speak (future update).
-   **Feedback:** Get an "Authority Score" and specific tips to improve your answers.
-   **Cheat Sheets:** Generate a one-page briefing document for any job description. Includes key talking points and questions to ask.

### 3. Opportunity Radar
Visualize your next move.
-   **Radar:** Jobs are plotted on a quadrant chart (Tech, Finance, Public, Creative).
-   **Distance:** Dots closer to the center are better matches for your skills.
-   **Salary:** Filter by salary range (£25k - £70k).
-   **Details:** Click a dot to see salary context, fit analysis, and apply links.

### 4. Re:Turn Hub
For professionals returning to work.
-   **CV Audit:** Specialized review for returners.
-   **Flex Negotiator:** Practice asking for flexible working arrangements. Includes a likelihood assessment and role-play simulations.
-   **90-Day Roadmap:** Generate a personalized plan for your first 3 months back in the role.

## Getting Started
1.  **Complete your CV:** Use the Builder or Audit tool first. This data powers the rest of the platform.
2.  **Check the Radar:** See what roles match your profile.
3.  **Practice:** Run a simulation for a role you like.
4.  **Prepare:** Generate a Cheat Sheet before your interview.
